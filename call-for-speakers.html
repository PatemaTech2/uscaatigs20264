Call for Speakers

Shape the Dialogue

Influence Investment. Drive Outcomes.

USCAATIGS 2026 invites distinguished policymakers, institutional investors, development finance leaders, corporate executives, subject matter experts, and industry innovators to contribute to a high-level, outcome-driven trade and investment platform.

This is not a general conference. It is a structured summit designed to align policy, capital, and bankable projects across Africa, the United States, and Canada.

Priority Thematic Tracks

1. Trade Policy & Market Access

AfCFTA implementation, U.S.–Africa and Canada–Africa trade frameworks

2. Investment & Capital Mobilization

Blended finance, risk mitigation, institutional capital deployment, PPPs

3. Energy & Energy Transition

Renewable project financing, grid modernization, industrial energy

4. Infrastructure & Industrialization

Transport corridors, industrial parks, manufacturing value chains

5. Mining & Critical Minerals

Responsible extraction, downstream processing, supply chains

6. Agribusiness & Food Systems

Agro-processing, climate-resilient agriculture, food security

7. Digital Economy & Innovation

Fintech, digital payments, cross-border trade, cybersecurity

8. Healthcare & Pharmaceutical

Local production, supply chain, health infrastructure

Speaker Formats

Ministerial Plenaries

Keynote addresses from government leaders

Panel Discussions

Moderated expert conversations

Fireside Chats

Intimate discussions with industry leaders

Who Should Apply

Government ministers and senior policymakers
Development finance institution executives
Institutional investors and fund managers
Export credit agencies
Multinational corporate leaders
Academic and policy thought leaders

What Speakers Receive

  • Full summit access
  • Profile listing on the official website
  • Participation in curated networking sessions
  • Eligibility for structured B2B engagement

Note: Travel and accommodation policies will be communicated upon confirmation. Speaking roles have no associated honorarium, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
