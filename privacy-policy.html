Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

USCAATIGS collects personal data during the registration process, including name, organization, role, email address, phone number, and payment information. This data is used solely for summit operations, communication about the event, and facilitating business connections between attendees.

Data Usage

Your personal data is used to:

  • • Process your registration and payment
  • • Communicate summit updates and information
  • • Facilitate B2B matchmaking and deal room scheduling
  • • Provide access to summit materials and platform

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Financial information is processed via secure, PCI-compliant payment processors.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties without your consent, except with service providers bound by confidentiality agreements who assist with summit operations. Delegate lists are shared only with explicit opt-in permission.

Cookies

We use cookies for website analytics (Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also opt-out of communications at any time. Contact privacy@uscaatigs2026.com to exercise these rights.

Data Retention

Personal data is retained for 5 years for institutional records and to facilitate participation in future USCAATIGS events.

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@uscaatigs2026.com
