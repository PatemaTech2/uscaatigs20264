Summit Overview

The Strategic Gateway to Global Prosperity.

USCAATIGS 2026 is a landmark global event designed to catalyze trade and investment between the United States, Canada, and Africa. We bring together heads of state, CEOs, and visionaries to forge sustainable economic partnerships.

Our mission is to create a dynamic ecosystem where capital meets opportunity, and innovation meets industrialization.

United States

Leveraging American technology and venture capital.

Canada

Expanding trade corridors and resource management.

Africa

Unlocking emerging markets and industrial potential.

Leadership & Vision

The Organizations Behind the Summit

U.S.-Africa Trade Commission

The U.S.-Africa Trade Commission (USAfriTrade) is dedicated to strengthening trade and investment relationships between the United States and African nations. Through policy advocacy and strategic partnerships, USAfriTrade facilitates sustainable economic growth across the continent.

Core Partner

ATESCA Canada

Africa Trade Export & Supermarket Canada (ATESCA) works to expose the rich culture of Africa through strategic trade collaboration with Canada, generating foreign exchange and tackling economic challenges while ensuring food security.

Strategic Partner
