Mission of U.S.-Africa Trade Commission

The U.S.-Africa Trade Commission (USAfriTrade) is dedicated to strengthening trade and investment relationships between the United States and African nations. Through policy advocacy, market access support, and strategic partnerships, USAfriTrade facilitates sustainable economic growth and development across the continent.

USAfriTrade works tirelessly to create opportunities for American businesses seeking to expand into African markets while supporting African nations in their economic development goals.

In Partnership With

ATESCA

Africa Trade Export & Supermarket Canada

Mission of Africa Trade Export & Supermarket Canada (ATESCA)

To expose the rich culture of Africa through strategic Trade & Investment collaboration with Canada with the intent to generate foreign exchange and create economic value while at the same time tackling graduate unemployment and food insecurity.
