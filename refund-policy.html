Cancellation Date Refund Amount

Substitutions

Substitutions are allowed at any time without fee. Please provide written notice to registration@uscaatigs2026.com with the substitute's details including full name, organization, email, and phone number.

Transfer to Future Year

Transfer to future year events is not available as each summit is an annual event with different themes and focus areas.

Cancellation by Organizer (Force Majeure)

If USCAATIGS is cancelled due to circumstances beyond our control (pandemic, natural disaster, political instability, etc.), full refund of registration fees will be provided within 30 days. Please note that travel and accommodation costs are not covered under this policy.

No-Show Policy

Failure to attend the summit does not entitle you to a refund. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover any potential travel disruptions.

Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 30 days of approval and refunded to the original payment method. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.
